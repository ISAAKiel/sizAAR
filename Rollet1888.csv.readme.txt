Explanation for data in Rollet1888.csv

The data is taken from the tables in Rollet (1888, p. 16-23). Further explanation mentions the shrinking of the measures by 2 mm within eight to ten months after the first measures in fresh condition. This differentiation is shown in the tables only for the femora. (Rollet 1888 p.24) Due to comparison with dry bones the table provides aditional colums without laterality for all bones with the mean for left and right measure minus 2 millimeters: ((mr + ml)/2) - 2 

Reference:
Rollet 1888, p. 16 ff.
